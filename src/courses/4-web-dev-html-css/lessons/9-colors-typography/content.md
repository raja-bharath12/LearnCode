## 1. Colors & Fonts
Hex, rgb, font-family, font-size, line-height.

## Practice Problems
### Easy
**Problem:** Blue text, lightgray bg
**Solution:**
 color:blue; background:lightgray;

### Medium
**Problem:** Centered heading, custom size paragraph
**Solution:**
 h1{text-align:center;} p{font-size:20px;}

### Hard
**Problem:** Google fonts, background, sizes
**Solution:**
 body{font-family:'Poppins'; background:#eef2f3;}
