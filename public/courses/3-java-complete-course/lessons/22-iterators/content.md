## 1. Iterator
Safe removal during iteration.

## Practice Problems
### Easy
**Problem:** Use Iterator.
**Solution:**
 `while(it.hasNext()) out.println(it.next());`

### Medium
**Problem:** Remove specific element.
**Solution:**
 `if(val == 5) it.remove();`

### Hard
**Problem:** ListIterator backwards.
**Solution:**
 `while(it.hasPrevious()) out.println(it.previous());`
